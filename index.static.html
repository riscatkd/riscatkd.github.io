HISTORY

Risca Tae Kwon-Do has a long and proud history of teaching Tae Kwon-Do for over 40 years. Formed in 1979, it is one of the longest-running clubs in Wales, and has counted Master black belts, Welsh internationals and world champions among its membership. The current instructors - Tom Wiseman (3rd degree black belt), Tom Wiseman Jr. (3rd degree black belt) and Simeon Bowen (3rd degree black belt) - have been Tae Kwon-Do practitioners for a combined total of 40 years.

Risca Tae Kwon-Do is part of the Tae Kwon-do Association of Great Britain (TAGB) – Europe’s largest single martial arts organisation, with over 25,000 members and over 600 clubs throughout the UK. All of our instructors are DBS checked, first aid trained and have been certified as instructors by 9th degree Grand Masters.
